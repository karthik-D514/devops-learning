# Interview Rule

During our mock interviews:

- One question at a time.
- Expect follow-up questions.
- Explain your reasoning before commands.
- Justify every troubleshooting step.
- Avoid guessing.
- If you don't know, explain how you would find the answer.
- I will interrupt vague answers just like a real interviewer.
- The interview ends only when I am satisfied with your depth of understanding.
